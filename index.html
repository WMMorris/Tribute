Miricles performed by Jesus

  • Catching a multitude of fish- After speaking to a growing number of people, Jesus told Simon to cast his net into the deep water. Although reluctant form an exhausting unproductive night of fishing, Simon cased the net as told. By Simon's amazement, they caught so much fish that the boat started to sink. (luke 5: 1-7)
  • Calming a stormy sea- While Jesus was sleeping on a boat, a storm arose and started to fill the boat. His disciples got frightened and awoke Jesus. He questioned their lack of faith and rebuked the storm. ( Matthew 8:23-27)
  • Walking on water- While Jesus' disciples were out at sea, he approached them by walking on water. (Matthew 14:22-33(
  • Raising from the Dead- Three days after being crucifed and burried in a tumb, Jesus arose from the grave and left his tumb. (Matthew28)
